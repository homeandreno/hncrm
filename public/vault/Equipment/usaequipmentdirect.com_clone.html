Welcome to USA Equipment Direct

USA Equipment Direct has been supplying commercial kitchens across the United States for over 20 years. Our experienced team works with restaurant owners, hotel operators, and foodservice professionals every day to help them find the right equipment at the right price.

We carry everything a professional kitchen needs: combi ovens, commercial steam ovens, fryers, processing equipment, restaurant ice machines, blast chillers, chest freezers, cold wells, commercial kitchen sinks, faucets, grease traps, work tables, commercial portable air conditioners, restaurant juicers, and much more from 400+ leading brands.

Based in Atlanta, GA, we ship anywhere in the continental United States and Canada. If you have questions about any product or need help putting together a full kitchen spec, our team is here to help.
